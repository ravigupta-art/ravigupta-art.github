ABOUT RAVI

Ravi Gupta is a storyteller. He works in the field of Comics, Illustration and Animation design.

Ravi has worked on projects ranging from storyboards, visual development, comics, motion graphics, and art direction. He holds a Bachelor's diploma in Animation Film Design from MIT Institute of Design.
